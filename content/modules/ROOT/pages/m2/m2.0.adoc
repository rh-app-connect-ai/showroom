=== The messaging scenario

Customers who have placed Purchase Orders may need to reach out to customer support — whether it's a complaint, a request to return items, or changes to an existing order. On the other side, support agents are ready to assist them.

The interesting part is that each side uses a *different* messaging platform:

* *Matrix* — where customers connect to submit their enquiries
* *Rocket.Chat* — where support agents work and respond

[TIP]
--
.What are these platforms?
[%collapsible]
====
*Matrix* is an open standard and communication protocol for real-time decentralised communication. It works seamlessly between different service providers.

*Rocket.Chat* is an open-source communications platform with high data protection standards. It enables real-time conversations between colleagues, other companies, or your customers across web, desktop, or mobile devices.
====
--

In the upcoming modules you'll use Apache Camel and Kafka to build the integrations that bridge both platforms — so that a message sent from one side appears seamlessly on the other.
